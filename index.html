About

ETT is a non-profit organization behind the series of high-caliber developer events in India.

We started in April 2010 by helping in organizing the first-ever RubyConfIndia conference in Bengaluru

On August 2016, we organized our first conference, Deccan RubyConf 2016.

Now we organizes around ten difference conferences and workshop throughout the calender year.
